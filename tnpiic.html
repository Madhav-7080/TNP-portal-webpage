Institution's Innovation Council (IIC)

The IIC, an initiative by the Ministry of Education, is our campus hub for fostering a culture of innovation. We organize workshops, hackathons, and idea competitions to inspire creative thinking and problem-solving among students.

Incubation Center

Our Incubation Center provides the ideal ecosystem to transform promising ideas into viable businesses. We support early-stage startups with mentorship, infrastructure, networking opportunities, and access to seed funding.
