Our Centers of Excellence are state-of-the-art facilities established in collaboration with leading technology companies. These centers provide students with direct access to industry-standard tools, official curriculum, and a platform to earn globally recognized certifications, making them highly valuable in the job market.

Our Industry Collaborations

AWS Academy

This center focuses on developing skills in cloud computing, providing students with the expertise needed to manage and deploy applications on the AWS cloud.

Key Areas:

  • Cloud Architecture & Infrastructure
  • Serverless Computing (Lambda)
  • Database Management (RDS, DynamoDB)
  • Cloud Security

Red Hat Learning Center

A specialized lab for mastering Linux administration and enterprise automation, preparing students for roles in system administration and DevOps.

Key Areas:

  • Linux System Administration (RHCSA)
  • Automation with Ansible
  • Containerization with Docker & Podman
  • Enterprise Virtualization
